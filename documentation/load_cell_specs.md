# Load Cell Datasheet Details (Model 1914A Femur Load Cell)

**Issue Date:** 5 December 2002

**Models:**  
1914A (and related models: HII 50M, HIII 95LM, HIII 50M, HIII 5F, THOR, BioSID, SID, EuroSID-1, SID-IIs, ES-2)  
_Note: Models 1914V and 1914 Femur Load Cells are identical except for bridge resistance._

- We have 1914A

**Applications:**

- Measures forces on the upper leg above the knee joint
- Useful for occupant protection systems (e.g., knee bolsters)
- Adaptable via removable/replacement end caps
- Contains six measurement channels: three forces and three moments
- Two 30-foot (9.15 m), 12-conductor, Teflon insulated, shielded cables exit the load cell (one for force channels, one for moment channels)
- With aluminum end caps (part #5632), can be used in the ES-2

- **Nonlinearity:** < 1% of full scale
- **Hysteresis:** < 1% of full scale
- **Crosstalk:** < 5% of full scale
- **Excitation:** 10 VDC nominal, up to 15 VDC max
- **Mass:** 1.043 kg (2.3 lb)
